title: The Internet Sucks...
slug: The-Internet-Sucks
date: 2004-06-15 07:44:24

10:54am - I think it could be time to take a break from the internet. I could put it down to a few reasons for why my life over the last year (or 10 years?) has been on my computer, as well as the fact I have nothing else to do and no where else to go during the week. But now that I am nearly finished school, I will not be spending as much time on the internet at all. I will find another addiction, get another habit. Try watching movies, playing games, pick up the guitar again, may even start reading books!   
  
What ever it is it will be off the computer. I feel stupid talking to people who don’t use computers/internet as much as I do. Trying to explain to them how good it is is very hard. Explaining how much of a community environment can be found on the internet. Through forums especially, you can talk to hundreds of people and hear what they have to say; sharing opinions, feelings and helping each other. It does feel good talking to a lot of people across the world, finding out that they really aren’t much different from me, that there are people who feel the same as I do. But what I try to remember is that the internet is not real life.   
  
These are not the people in my life, the people that matter. This is just another way of life, but not the primary one. I sometimes feel that I forget that. Forget how to interact with real people. No ‘LOLs’ and shit like that.
