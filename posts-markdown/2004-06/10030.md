I've just spent the last hour or so looking through various communities here on LiveJournal and I gotta say I think its really depressing. Yes, I spend a lot of time on my computer, and the internet, but I dont go posting pictures of myself and information about me all over the net for others to read in the hope that I will quite literally be "accepted into their community." Reading about the information that people post on these things, hoping that they will be 'rated highly' or loved by all. And then there are the people who reject others saying stuff like, "no, just no, cos im a bitch and i dont like you," or "no, your ugly" etc. WHY!   
  
Why do people look to be accepted by random people half way across the world. These people dont mean shit! Its not these people that matter to you. Will you be happier if these people find you attractive, love your interests and appreciate that you promote their community only so it can go on again in this never ending circle of virtual hatred, bitchiness and love.   
  
If you want to do it, fine. Just know that these people _do_ mean shit. They dont know the _real_ you! Nor do they _really_ care,
