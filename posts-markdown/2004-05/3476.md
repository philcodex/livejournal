Got to the Front Page just in time to for the sound check. They even sounded good then! They went on at about 9:30. Played 6 songs in total incluging 2 great instrumentals. Among the songs they played were Fire by Jimi Hendrex and Another Brick in the Wall (Part II) by Pink Floyd. Both of these covers were excellent! Amazing to listen to whilst stoned! They truly are a band whose name i will spread! I AM joint #2 Fan!!
