So i found out the other day that some guy from [BT](http://www.bt.com) had been round at the house and spoke to my dad. He managed to pursuade him to get rid of [NTL](http://www.ntl.com) and "go back to BT". I dont know what the guy said, but i would like to know. But its all good! I dont really care about the [NTL phone line](http://www.ntlhome.co.uk/ntl_telephone/), its the [channels](http://www.ntlhome.co.uk/ntl_tv/) (few as they are) and the [NTL phone line](http://www.ntlhome.co.uk/ntl_telephone/), its the [Internet](http://www.ntlhome.co.uk/ntl_internet/) that i care about!  
  
I think we will definately stick with NTL for the internet. [The fact that they are increasing all the speeds](http://www.theregister.co.uk/2004/04/26/ntl_telewest_broadband/) is the main thing making me want to stay! According to the article on [The Register](http://www.theregister.co.uk) they are increasing the 150k to 300k (thats me!), the 600k is to increase to 750k and 1mb moving up to 1.5mb, and all for the same price as before (except the 1mb which will be increased by £3)!   
  
Though they have strong competition from [BT who are reducing their prices by 70%](http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/business/3709949.stm) and Telewest who are [increasing their speeds and freezing their prices](http://www.theregister.co.uk/2004/04/26/telewest_broadband_cable/) (which are less than NTL's!), [they are also going to provide the first 3MB connection in the UK!](http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/technology/3646825.stm)   
This is all to happen in the summer!  
  
As for the TV. We're getting [SKY!!](http://www.sky.com) So im pretty happy about that! Apparently we're getting about [200 channels!](http://www.sky.com/skycom/packages/channel/0,,1,00.html)   
  
Its all good!!
