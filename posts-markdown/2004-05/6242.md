Yesterday my brother got the new James Bond game for the PS2, [Everything or Nothing](http://www.eagames.com/official/007/everythingornothing/us/home.jsp). It is completely different from the last one, [Nightfire](http://www.eagames.com/official/007/nightfire/us/home.jsp), which was amazing! I havnt yet played it that much, but what i have played of it, and seen, i have really liked. It is apparently a lot harder than Nightfire, but it does look like it could be better. The graphics are definately better!  
  
In multiplayer, two players can play co-operatively to complete a set of levels specifically for multiplayer. What i do like about this game is that it combines a lot of different types of play. For example, on many levels you are driving a car and others you are on foot. Thumbs Up to EA Games.
