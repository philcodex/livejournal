Ive been back from the United States of America for 2 weeks.  
  
It was the best time of my life! The difference in lifestyle, culture and great people!  
  
I can say that a few of my best friends live in America and I cant wait to see em again!!  
  
I will be spending the next year saving to go back next summer!
