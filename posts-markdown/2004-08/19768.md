When im stoned i end up really thinking about things in a lot of detail. I would be lying in bed with a million thoughts running through my head. I often want to write them down cos i know i wont remember it in the morning. So last night i got outta bed, searched for paper and pen and started writing down what i was thinking. It was one of those times where i got really depressed for a while. I lay there and wished that i had a mobile. I got a massive urge to send a txt message. To talk to someone... Its things like this that give me real determination to do what i want to do...
