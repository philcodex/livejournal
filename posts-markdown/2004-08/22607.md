Today i worked from 9-6. Dad left the house at about 8am, i got up at about 8am. I left at 8:30. I went out the back door. Dad had also gona out the back door. He hadnt opened the main front door. Mum and bro are in portstewart. I closed the back door, walked round to the front of the house and said "SHIT!" Yup i noticed the door hadnt been opened. I didnt have the key for it. I would have to get into the house via secret entrance.   
  
At lunch i came home from work cos i had no money and needed food. I squeezed through secret entrance. Luckily i managed. I ate. I left for work again, feeling much better with lots of food in me.   
  
While i was home i noticed a letter addressed to me. I opened it and it was from Dunnes Stores.
