The movie in question is [Wonderland](http://uk.imdb.com/title/tt0335563/). In short - this was one awesome, fucked up, truthful mind-blowing, wonderful film. Its about John Holmes, a legendary porn star in the 60s and 70s. The story revolves around the murder of 4 people in a house on Wonderland Avenue on July 1, 1981. We hear the story from both David Lind, one of the 'lads' and John himself. Both giving slightly different stories as to what happened in an attempt to discover who killed, or who should be convicted of committing this quadruple homicide.   
  
The film reveals the seedy world of drugs and violence in such great detail and was a thril to watch!
