But being typically her, she just went on and on and on and on and on about it all weekend. This is exactly why i am so glad that ive moved out! I listen to her, a little, when shes around, and when im not near her i completely block her out and ignore it all, in a way. On a lighter note, I went to the cinema to see Shark Tale, then met ronan and ronan for a few games of pool and couple of drinks. And i got to sleep in my own bed, which is a lot better than the one i have here, though this one isnt too bad. I also have nicer pillows at home and im going to bring one of them back with me next time im home.   
  
On sunday we went to grannys and... well didnt do anything that exciting really; watched friends (yay(!)).

Its really anoying when you're playing games or watching tv/dvd.
