Disneyland

So i've been lazy lately and not bothered to update this - but i want to and am definately going to do it now!

We got back from Paris on Wednesday. I great 6 days!

We left on Thursday at about 12. Arrived at about 4pm. Got coach from the Airport to Disneyland. I knew we had arrived when i saw the first huge hotel, then the rest soon after - they are massive! We arrived at ours eventually. The Kryiad was the hotel we stayed at. Very nice, very big!

The first night we just took the bus from the hotel to the Disney Village (free entry area with clubs, bars, restaurants and shops), just took a look around the place.

The next day we got up early for breakfast at 9am. This consisted of a few croissants, yoghurt and another bread type thing or two.

On the first day we went to the most recent of the two Parks: Walt Disney Studios.

Disney Studios was great. Lots of special effects, rides and adventures into the world of Disney, that sorta stuff. The weather was amazing which added to it all!


I went on the Aerosmith Rock 'n Roller Coaster - which was amazing. I dont usually do well on rollercoasters, but this was great. It lined up and counted down from 3. Then it went off at high speed into the darkness. The track went all over the place in what seemed like a very small room in almost complete darkness with the occasional bit of blue and silver track.

Next we went on the Armageddon (special effects) thing. It was... well not that good really, just a room that shook around, a few flames and lots of smoke.

Next was the Studio Tram Tour. This tour brought you round the back area of the park. Some very nice effects, shown here!

One of the best shows was at the Moteurs Action Stunt Show.
