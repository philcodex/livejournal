Gaye Paris

So on Sunday 29th August we packed our stuff and placed it in the safety of the hotel while we went off for our 3rd and final day of Disneyland enjoyment.

After a few hours of wandering around the park on my own trying to take it all in while my dad and and bro went on the Pirates of the Carribbean ride again (for the third time) we finally went back to the hotel to get our stuff and then to the train station.

Off the train and onto the appriate metro line to the nearest station from the hotel.

Hotel Novanox. It wasnt as good as the one in disneyland, or as big by far - but it was still nice and the people who ran it were pretty nice.

On the first evening i didnt bring camera. We went to the Eiffel Tower, which looked amazing all lite up.

The next day we got up early for breakfast and headed out to Notre Dame. Just a huge cathederal really - very nice on the inside! No flash photography they say.

The huge stained glass window inside the church:

Later we went to Eiffel's Tower - this time went up it, right to the top!



What a view

Later we took a boat trip along the River Seine - which is where i took this pic from

One of the beautiful bridges along the river

Day two - We left the hotel at about 11 and I went around on my own until meeting the parents again at about 4pm. I went to a History Museum and tried to go the Picasso Museum but it was closed on Tuesdays - really anoyed at that.

Hotel de Ville - I love the buildings in Paris - everyone is so spectacular!

Moulin Rouge

Finally - The Lourve. Unfortunately we didnt get to go in as it was closed when we went and this was on the final evening. Such a beautiful place!




Yes i had a great time and will definately be going back!!!
