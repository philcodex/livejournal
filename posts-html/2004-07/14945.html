Shrek 2

I felt a little lost having not seen all of the original movie. I remember watching it shortly after it was out on dvd, but getting a little bored half way through - not even half way through in fact. I just wasnt in the mood. Ever since then i wish i had seen it. I guess i just havent had the chance. But ever since i saw the trailers for the sequal I've been in fits of laughter and eagerly awaiting its release.

The Google was a bundle of laughs the whole way through. I loved it. The soundtrack was spectacular. It was full of surprises and kept me hanging on for the entire duration. i have heard a few people say that its not better than the original... i have seen some clips since my first brief encouter and i regret not finding it as funny then as i do now. I feel there is something missing having not seen it. My goal is to ultimately see the original and then i think i will feel a lot better. Definately one (or two) to add to any great movie collection!
