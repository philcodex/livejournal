I killed a wasp!

I used all my power and strength and managed to kill a wasp that got into my room and scared the shit outta me floating around my lamp beside me. My heart begins to pound when it flys out of sight. Armed with a large piece of cardboard and an pen i waited until it was crawling on the table, then i hit it with the edge of the cardboard and stabbed it a couple of times with the pen. When i was sure it was dead i put it onto the cardboard, threw it out the window and that was the end of that.

Time for bed i should think...
