WonDerland

Up at 1:30, breakfast at 2:30. Town 5:00. Ronan still asleep! Went with Rach to play pool. Big American tables with crap cues. Fries at Burger King. Cigarettes. Ronan finally turns up at 7:30, fresh outta bed!

We went to Auntie Annies for a bit then back to Burger King to smoke and converse until the movie started at 9:15.

The movie in question is Wonderland. In short - this was one awesome, fucked up, truthful mind-blowing, wonderful film. Its about John Holmes, a legendary porn star in the 60s and 70s. The story revolves around the murder of 4 people in a house on Wonderland Avenue on July 1, 1981. We hear the story from both David Lind, one of the 'lads' and John himself. Both giving slightly different stories as to what happened in an attempt to discover who killed, or who should be convicted of committing this quadruple homicide.

The film reveals the seedy world of drugs and violence in such great detail and was a thril to watch!


Great film, great day outdoors!
